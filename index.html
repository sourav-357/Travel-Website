Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about traveling with us

What is included in your travel packages?

Our comprehensive travel packages include:

  • Accommodation: Carefully selected hotels, resorts, or boutique properties
  • Transportation: Airport transfers, inter-city transport, and local transportation
  • Guided Tours: Professional local guides and entrance fees to attractions
  • Meals: Breakfast daily, plus selected lunches and dinners as specified
  • Activities: Pre-planned experiences and excursions
  • Support: 24/7 customer service and emergency assistance
  • Insurance: Basic travel insurance (upgrade options available)

Some premium packages also include international flights, travel insurance, and additional perks like spa treatments or exclusive experiences.

Can I customize my travel itinerary?

Yes! We offer flexible customization options for all our packages. Our travel experts will work with you to create the perfect itinerary that matches your preferences and budget.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation policies. You can cancel up to 30 days before departure for a full refund, 15-30 days for 75% refund, and 7-15 days for 50% refund.

Do you provide travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options. Our insurance covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost luggage, and other travel-related incidents.

How do I book a trip?

Booking is easy! Simply browse our packages, select your preferred dates, fill out the booking form, and make payment. Our team will confirm your booking within 24 hours.
